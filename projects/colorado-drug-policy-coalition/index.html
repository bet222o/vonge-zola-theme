Colorado Drug Policy Coalition

Statewide Harm Reduction policy.

The Colorado Drug Policy Coalition (CODPC) is an alliance of organizations and individuals working to make our communities healthier and safer by promoting enlightened drug policy in Colorado based on evidence, human rights, social justice, public health best practices, and the principles of Harm Reduction.

I am the Policy Director of CODPC, a statewide coalition that formed in response to Colorado's 2023 Overdose Prevention Center (OPC) bill and that advances anti-carceral drug policy across the state. OPCs are proven to save lives, make neighborhoods safer, and connect people with substance use disorders to vital services and treatment.

CODPC works on

  • Decriminalizing drug possession and use as a public-health-first policy stance.
  • Coalition advocacy alongside the Harm Reduction Action Center, Right Response Colorado and drug policy partners statewide.
