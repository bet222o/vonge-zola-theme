**High Walls** is a community-led photovoice project on incarceration,
mental health, and substance use, exhibited at the [**Redline
Contemporary Art Center**](https://www.redlineart.org/high-walls-summit)
in Denver on **August 17, 2025**. The project was co-led by Dr. LeMasters and Betsy Craft alongside community
advisory board members Macht, Walker, Moffett, McConnellogue, and Wise with lived experience of substance use, mental health and incarceration in the Denver metro area.Humanizing Drug Overdoses in Colorado: A Liberatory Harm Reduction Comic Made with Sex Workers and People Who Use Drugs” in the Journal of American Folklore
