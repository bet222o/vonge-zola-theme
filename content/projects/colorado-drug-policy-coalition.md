I'm the **Policy Director** at the **Colorado Drug Policy Coalition
(CDPC)**, a statewide coalition that formed in response to Colorado's 2023
Overdose Prevention Center (OPC) bill and that advances data-driven harm
reduction and drug policy reform across the state.

[Visit codpc.org →](https://www.codpc.org)
[Follow @coloradodpc on Instagram →](https://www.instagram.com/coloradodpc)

### What CODPC works on

- **Decriminalizing** drug possession and use as a public-health-first
  policy stance.
- **Coalition advocacy** alongside the Harm Reduction Action Center,
  Right Response Colorado, C2PAN, and partners statewide.
